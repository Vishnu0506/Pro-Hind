    Greetings from our website, which serves as a forum for the positive exchange of ideas around the difficult subject of Hindus experiencing prejudice or oppression in situations when they come into contact with people or groups who identify as "peaceful people". In an effort to foster understanding throughout communities, we want to establish a forum for courteous and candid discussions about the realities faced by Hindus in these circumstances. Our goal is to address this subject with compassion, decency, and a dedication to fostering harmonious cohabitation.

    Our Mission,

    We acknowledge that religious conflict and discrimination-related concerns can be extremely delicate. Our goal is to present a fair and knowledgeable analysis of this issue. We want to bring attention to the experiences of Hindus who have encountered difficulties when interacting with "peaceful people," not to generalise or demonise any particular group. We hope to establish a forum where people may respectfully and intelligently converse by utilising in-depth research, firsthand accounts, professional viewpoints, and historical background.

    Key Features:

    • In-Depth Analysis: Our website offers a range of articles, reports, and analyses that delve into the historical, political, and social contexts of the challenges faced by Hindus. We seek to provide a comprehensive understanding of the complex dynamics at play.
    • Personal Stories: We give a voice to individuals who have experienced discrimination, oppression and are willing to share their personal narratives. These stories provide a human perspective on the issue.
    • Expert Opinions: We feature the insights of experts, scholars, and researchers who offer their informed perspectives on the topic, helping to contextualize and analyze the situation.

    Promoting Dialogue and Understanding:

    We believe that through open and informed conversations, we can bridge divides and foster understanding among different communities. Our website aims to encourage empathy, tolerance, and peaceful coexistence by bringing people together to explore this challenging issue from multiple angles.

    Conclusion:

    We cordially welcome you to browse our website and to work with us to build a more accepting and peaceful world. We seek to contribute to a more sympathetic and knowledgeable global discourse by concentrating on comprehending the difficulties faced by Hindus.
